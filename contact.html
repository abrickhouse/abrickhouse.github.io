Acetocholine

ACh is released by peripheral motor neurons for skeletal movement. If it doesn't release-- paralysis

Glutamate

Widely distributed excitatory neurotransmitter, learning, memory. Too much?-- seizure

GABA

primary inhibitor neurotransmitter, slows down neuroprocessing, depressant, alcohol, sleep

Norepinephrine

Also known as noradreniline, physiological arousal, stimulant, heart rate increase, on-edge, excited. If it doesn't release-- depression

Serotonin

mood regulation, sleep and wake cycles, hunger, anger

Dopamine

Controls motor planning and voluntary movement. Also implicated in reward processing, incentivising, and pleasure

Endorphins

natural pain reliever (emotional or physical)
